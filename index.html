about me

Thanks for dropping by, as you probably already know my name is Kendra Hallam. I am a never-ending student of coding.

I have spent the past 10 years as a Digital Production Artist at Universal Engraving, Inc. During my duration at UEI, I gained knowledge creating textures, manipulating images and typography while using Illustrator and Photoshop.

Prior to my experience at Universal Engraving Inc., I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree in Commercial Graphics from Pittsburg State University, in Pittsburg, Kansas. Following my BST degree, I began seeking a Master of Science in Technical Teacher Education. I guess you could say I can never get enough of education.

I am currently seeking a Full Stack Development certification through the University of Kansas. Upon completion, I hope to obtain a position as Front-End Developer.
