about me

I spent the past 10 years as a Production Artist at Universal Engraving, Inc. Within the 10 years I have gained knowledge with creating textures and manipulating pieces of artwork and typography through using Illustrator and Photoshop.

Prior to my experience at UEI, I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree in Commercial Graphics in 2004 from Pittsburg State University. Following my BST degree in 2004, I began seeking a Master of Science in Technology Education, where I hope to be able to instruct others in the future in the world of technology.

I am currently seeking a Web Development certification through the University of Kansas.
